Census Age Calculator

Searching for your ancestors in the UK Census data?



This tool calculates the census records in which your ancestors might be recorded, and their age at the time it was taken. The UK census is usually taken every ten years and is made available to the public 100 years after its commission. The table below shows the records currently available and gives a brief overview of their contents.

All of the censuses and the 1939 register can be searched at Ancestry.ca and at Find my Past except the 1921 census which is searchable only through Find my Past. Both sites require paid subscriptions.
